This mod replaces all the music in the game with various Sonic the Hedgehog tracks, the theme was mostly supposed to be from SA2, but not a whole lot of it fits sadly, but SA1, StH, SU, and SatBK fit very well (and some fun non-sonic surprise tracks as well) 
As a heads up, I chose -most- of the songs to fit the mood of the song it's replacing, not specifically trying to tie character themes to other character themes (I.E. Amy's theme not being for Kairi, or Shadow's theme for Riku, neither of them fit well IMO)
***BIG UPDATE: Now all the Organization XIII members, the 1000 Heartless Battle, the Riku Summit Fight, and the FF Ally Run after HB Demyx have their own unique themes!
Only 1 song outside of the Pre-Rendered FMV's I can't change because it's also technically an FMV sequence, which is the Sora Flashbacks in the Roxas tutorial.
